## Color Scheme

### Card States:

**Default:**
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Border: Light Blue (#8FABD4) - 2px
- Shadow: None

**Hover:**
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Border: Dark Blue (#4A70A9) - 2px
- Shadow: 0 12px 24px rgba(74, 112, 169, 0.25)
- Transform: translateY(-8px)

**Active (Click):**
- Transform: translateY(-4px)

### Text Colors:
- **Title:** Dark Blue (#4A70A9) - Bold 700
- **Description:** Gray (#666666) - Regular
- **Badge:** White text on Dark Blue background
